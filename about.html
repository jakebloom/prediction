About

My name is Jake Bloom and this is the JB Prediction 100 Index. I called it the JB Prediction 100 because that's my name and because I make the rules on this website.

This Index tracks the top 100 markets on Polymarket, excluding sports and contracts that expire in the next 24 hours, and weights them by volume. The Index rebalances every day.

You can think of this as a weighted average price of a contract on Polymarket. Or you can think of this as a tracker of entropy.
